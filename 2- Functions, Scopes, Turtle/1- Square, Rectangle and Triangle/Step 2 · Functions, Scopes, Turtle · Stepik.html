What

Python library for work with graphics if you want to draw something

You manipulate a "turtle" with a pen

You move the turtle pen draws some lines

This will draw a square

turtle.shape("turtle")

Try it

 

There are two types of commands

Move

turtle.forward(50)

move 50 pixels along the current direction of a turtle

Turn

turtle.left(90)

means turn change the orientation of a turtle by 90 degrees to the left

 

Degrees

Specify direction

There are 360 degrees in total

* draw a square with side 100 pixels

* draw a rectangle: horizontal sides are 100 and vertical sides are 50 pixels
